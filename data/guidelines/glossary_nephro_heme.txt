# Nephrology / Hematology Glossary

AKI: Acute kidney injury; sudden drop in kidney function.
CKD: Chronic kidney disease.
Hematuria: Blood in urine.
Proteinuria: Protein in urine.
Hyperkalemia: High potassium; risk of arrhythmia.
Uremia: Toxin buildup in kidney failure.
Dialysis indications: Refractory hyperkalemia, acidosis, fluid overload, uremic symptoms.
Thrombocytopenia: Low platelets; bleeding risk.
Leukopenia: Low white blood cells; infection risk.
Anemia: Low hemoglobin.

Anticoagulant: Blood thinner (e.g., heparin, warfarin).
Antiplatelet: Stops platelets (e.g., aspirin, clopidogrel).
