# Infectious Disease Glossary

Neutropenic fever: Fever with neutrophils <500; treat as emergency infection.
Sepsis: Life-threatening organ dysfunction from infection.
Septic shock: Sepsis with persistent hypotension requiring vasopressors.
CAP: Community-acquired pneumonia.
HAP: Hospital-acquired pneumonia.
UTI: Urinary tract infection.
Pyelonephritis: Kidney infection.
Bacteremia: Bacteria in blood.
Empiric antibiotics: Initial broad antibiotics before cultures.
Source control: Remove/drain infection source (abscess, line).
Hypotension: Low blood pressure; red flag in infection.
Lactate: Marker of tissue hypoperfusion; elevated in sepsis.
